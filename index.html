Welcome to Munchkin Match

Munchkin Match simplifies the process of finding childcare for children aged 4 and under. We help parents locate daycare facilities quickly, compare their features, and make an informed decision easily.

The Problem Space

Finding a daycare facility can be overwhelming, time-consuming, and unorganized. Parents struggle with numerous options, varying pricing, child-to-caregiver ratios, and different curriculums. Munchkin Match addresses this by consolidating the search and comparison process into one seamless platform.

Features

  • Search for daycare facilities near you
  • Compare facilities based on pricing, ratios, and curriculum
  • View detailed facility profiles with photos and reviews
  • Save favorite facilities for future reference
  • Access waiting list and schedule tour options
